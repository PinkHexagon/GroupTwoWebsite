About Us

Somerset Country Preserves uses only the freshest fruits and ingredients, from local farmers around Somerset and the rest of Britain. Our journey began in 2024 on a local farm producing jams, spreads and honey to name a few! Have a browse of our shop to see our product range and delicious treats!

Here is a picture of our team on the farm where it all began! We have helped loads of companies within the catering industry, restaurants and more! We are based in a little village in Somerset just down from Dog Lane. We have a local shop where we sell our produce to our customers and we look forward to welcoming you to our shop!
